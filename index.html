RanChat Child Safety Standards

RanChat is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Our Commitment

RanChat takes child safety seriously and has implemented comprehensive measures to prevent, detect, and respond to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation on our platform.

Safety Measures

  • Age Verification: Users must be 18+ to use our platform
  • Content Moderation: Automated and manual review of all shared content
  • Reporting System: Easy-to-use in-app reporting for safety concerns
  • User Blocking: Users can block and report inappropriate behavior
  • Content Filtering: AI-powered detection of inappropriate content
  • Account Suspension: Immediate action against policy violations

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that may involve child sexual abuse or exploitation:

  • Use our in-app reporting feature immediately
  • Contact us directly at the email below
  • Report to local authorities if necessary

Content Policy

RanChat strictly prohibits:

  • Sharing or requesting child sexual abuse material
  • Grooming or sexual communication with minors
  • Any form of child exploitation
  • Inappropriate content involving minors

Compliance

RanChat complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • Reporting to regional and national authorities
  • Cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  • Regular safety audits and updates
  • Staff training on child safety protocols
